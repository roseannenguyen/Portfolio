Work Day Scheduler Application

The Work Day Scheduler Application is a simple work day calendar from 9AM to 7PM. The block is color coded to indicate whether it occurs in the past (gray), present (red) or future green.

Languages and Technologies: HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Moment.js.
